Life at Finquest

Our way of doing things

  • Truly global

    All our teams are geographically dispersed. They collaborate with, and learn from each other on a daily basis.

  • Diverse by design

    We’re a company built on inclusion. We encourage everyone to bring their unique culture, values and perspective to the mix, because this originates the best ideas.

  • Inately flexible

    Where you’re located isn’t as important as the results you bring. We take a hybrid approach, where staff can work remotely for up to three days a week.

  • Environmentally minded

    Remote working, a restriction on unnecessary travel, and fully digital offices are helping us go greener. We’re also making our data centers air-con free and relying on clean energy, in a bid to further reduce our carbon footprint.

  • Realising Potential

    At Finquest we prioritise personal growth and development. Our onboarding process is comprehensive, and all employees receive an L&D budget they can spend as they see fit.

  • Compassionate

    Employees are actively encouraged to build supportive communities within the business. And, as well as a charity day, Finquest hosts an annual “mental health day”, where everyone downs tools to focus on their wellbeing.
